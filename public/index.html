Is PG safe?

Propylene Glycol is the primary ingredient in the majority of e­liquids and e­cigarette cartridges on the marketplace today. Most e­liquid contains at least 80% propylene glycol. This is the ingredient that produces the smoke­like vapor when the e­cigarette is exhaled.Products with Propylene Glycol can be found in various common items around your house. A few examples include: Pharmaceuticals – Oral, injectable and topical formulations, Beauty products including makeup, mousse, shampoo, bubble bath, after shave, and deodorant, baby wipes, Pet food, Asthma inhalers, Because of its low chronic oral toxicity, propylene glycol was classified by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration as “generally recognized as safe” (GRAS).
